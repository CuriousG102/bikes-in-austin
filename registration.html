Register Your Bike

Once your bicycle is registered, it will remain in the system until you notify us that your bicycle has been disposed of. If you sell or dispose of your bicycles in any way please update your report by submitting a supplement to your original report. When adding a supplement to the registration, you will need to re-enter personal information. You will receive a copy of your original report once it has been processed. Please keep a copy of this report and the case number for your records. Only 1 bike may be registered at a time.
